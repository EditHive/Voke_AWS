## 1. Executive Summary

**Vision**: Transform interview preparation through AI-powered personalized coaching, making professional success accessible to everyone.

**Mission**: Empower job seekers with cutting-edge AI technology to practice, improve, and excel in interviews through realistic simulations and actionable feedback.

**Value Proposition**: The only interview preparation platform that combines video analysis, voice AI coaching, adaptive text interviews, peer practice, and personalized learning paths - all powered by advanced AI to deliver professional-grade feedback at a fraction of traditional coaching costs.
